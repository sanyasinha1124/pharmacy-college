About College of Pharmacy

Founded in 2000,College of Pharmacy has been a pioneer in pharmaceutical education, combining rigorous academics with hands-on training to prepare students for successful careers in pharmacy and pharmaceutical sciences.

Our faculty includes renowned researchers and practitioners who are committed to student success. With state-of-the-art facilities and strong industry partnerships, we provide an unparalleled educational experience.

Our Academic Programs

We offer comprehensive programs designed to meet the evolving needs of the pharmaceutical industry

Bachelor of Pharmacy (B.Pharm)

Our 4-year B.Pharm program provides a strong foundation in pharmaceutical sciences with hands-on laboratory experience and industry exposure through internships.

  • Duration: 4 Years
  • Intake: 100 Students
  • Eligibility: 10+2 with PCB/M
  • Approved by PCI
Program Details

Master of Pharmacy (M.Pharm)

Specialize in Pharmaceutical Chemistry, Pharmacology, Pharmaceutics, or Pharmacy Practice with our 2-year M.Pharm program.

  • Duration: 2 Years
  • Specializations: 4
  • Eligibility: B.Pharm
  • Research Focus
